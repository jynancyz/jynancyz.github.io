Servicenow Internship

i

A summer internship completed at Servicenow in San Diego, CA.

Goal: complete individual research project and assist with continuing user tests.

What I did

♦   Completed user tests on upcoming product releases
♦   Attended multiple customer visits in CA and FL
♦   Acted as primary researcher on project team
♦   Compiled user interview insights in low-fidelity wireframes with UX designers

&

What I learned

♦   Multiple notetakers on user tests prevent missing key insights.
♦   Observations in the field allow for in-depth notes on user behavior.
♦   Diverse teams demand sufficient updates.



The Activities:

1. User Testing: Internal user testing on new releases and insight gathering.



2. Wallboards: Discovery user interviews and cluster analysis of key points.

Example questions: "How are you involved in civic engagement?",
"What do you think of the civic engagement processes that are currently used?"



3. Wallboards: Ideation with team on insights.

We found that most civic issues circle around three key elements: Scale, Transparency, and Convergence. These were supported by transcribed quotes from the conducted interviews with civic leaders and planners. The data was split into three categories concerning: Participation, Retention, and Openness of Process which line up with the civic issues found.
